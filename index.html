Work

Click here to watch my presentation

Concierge NYC - I personally built an app that allows a full time employee to request time off. The manager would receive a text message notifying him and can respond with a simple “Yes” to approve for another employee to claim using a cool API “Twilio”. This app helps a manager find relief work under short periods of time and also makes the process of requesting time off for the employees much easier. I used JavaScript as well as VueJs to create a smooth working front end. PostgreSQL database and the backend of this application uses Ruby on Rails.

Currently I am working with a software development team in an agile environment. I added features to the real CRM which Actualize uses to manage its prospective applicants. The CRM allows a user to call and text a lead through the Twilio API. I also worked on features involving its UI, including an internal restructuring that transformed the app into a Single Page Application, making it much easier to use. This app uses Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and VueJS.

In addition, I have built a YouTube style website using React with Redux. Along the way, I learned many fundamental concepts such as functional components, class components and state.

About Me

Born and raised in Brooklyn, I have watched how much the borough has transformed and undergone rapid gentrification. I appreciate the change.

Career

Working as a concierge in midtown Manhattan for 11 years gave me the opportunity to create many social relationships. I was able to learn from so many different backgrounds and cultures as well as different levels of success, all of which are priceless. It has helped to shape my personality in a very positive way. Starting a career in web development wasn’t easy for me. This is my second attempt getting back into it after a failed first attempt at trying to self teach through multiple sources. I became overwhelmed and thought I wasn’t smart enough. I realize now I was not practicing a growth mindset, I’m very happy that I did. I attended the online live course at Actualize which I would highly recommend, I learned Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, Css, HTML, VueJs, and since then React, Node.Js and even a little Python and Django. Along the way I’ve also taken up electronic music production. In the past 2-3 years I have learned how to work with Ableton live 9, Vst’s like Serum, Massive, Fab Filter, and many others. I couldn’t help but see similarities between both coding and electronic music production.

Hobbies

I’m big on outdoor activities as well. During the warmer months, I try to squeeze in a few bike tours and hiking trips. It’s a great way to meet new people with similar interest and experience New York in a fun way.
